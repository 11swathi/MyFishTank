3 little Fish Tanks

We’re Swathi & Vignesh — proud parents of 3 little fish tanks 🐠

We have three beautiful tanks filled with big and small fishes, vibrant red and green plants, and countless little details we’ve fallen in love with along the way. From visiting aquarium auctions to spending weekends rearranging plants and watching our fishes swim around, this hobby has become something truly special to us.


For us, fishkeeping is more than just tanks and filters — it’s peaceful, calming, and honestly a little addictive. There’s something magical about sitting in front of a glowing aquarium after a long day and getting lost in this tiny world underwater. This page is a small glimpse into our aquatic journey, our favorite tanks, and the hobby that keeps growing one fish at a time.

The Story Behind our Fish Tanks 🐠

About Us

Vignesh has always had a soft spot for fishes ever since he was a little kid. After we got married, he would tell me stories about the fishes he had growing up and how much he loved watching them swim around peacefully. So, for his birthday in 2024, I decided to surprise him with a tiny blue betta fish. We named him Phineas, and honestly, that one little fish started it all.

At first, we got a simple little 10-gallon tank just for Phineas. But somewhere between decorating the tank, choosing plants, and spending way too much time staring at the water, our little gift turned into a full-blown hobby. When we moved into a bigger house, Vignesh immediately started dreaming bigger too. One tank became three, and before we knew it, we had added a massive 55-gallon tank and a beautiful 45-gallon setup to the family.

Now our home is basically part aquarium, part therapy center. What started as a birthday surprise became something we both genuinely love building together — one fish tank at a time.
